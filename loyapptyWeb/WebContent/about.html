About Us

Every business has important messages to communicate to customers. Loyappty gives businesses, of all sizes, a cost-effective and very simple way to communicate with its customers directly to their mobile phone. Loyappty combines the effectiveness of SMS (text messaging) marketing with the convenience of mobile applications.

Getting started in Loyappty is very simple:

1) Register your business. You can sign-up entering your business information, or using your Facebook or Twitter account.

2) Get customers to opt-in to your messages. Once you complete the registration a QR Code and text code is generated that uniquely identifies your business. A promotional flyer with your business logo, name, description, QR Code, and your custom message is automatically generated. Print it, and have your customers scan it with their mobile phone to opt-in. They can also search for Loyappty in their smartphone App Marketplace and download it completely free.

3) Communicate with them. You can send a message to an individual customer, to a group, or to all.

How does Loyappty compare to other smilar services? There are no other similar services. There are a number of “deal of the day”, “coupon of the day” type services in the market, but none in which the business is in total control of the relationship with its customers. Through Loyappty each business decides what and when to communicate with its customers.
