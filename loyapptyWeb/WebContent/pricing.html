Pricing

Absolutely free to Customers!

The Loyappty Mobile App is complete free to download. There is no advertising in Loyappty.

The registration is free, no credit card, no obligation, no setup fees. When you decide Loyappty is a right fit for your business, choose from one of the subscription options below.

When registering, the account is setup with 10 free credits to start Loyappting risk free. Free credits will be valid for 2 months.

 

Subscription Plans

Monthly Annual Custom

Includes up to 1,000 messages per month Includes up to 15,000 messages per year

Additional messages, top-up, can be purchased in buckets:

  • 2,500 messages for $10
  • 15,000 messages for $50
  • 35,000 messages for $100

Each message sent to customers uses one credit.

Credits do not expire.

Either a monthly or annual subscription is required.

Businesses can cancel or transition to next plan at any time.

 

Payments in loyappty are securely and conveniently processed through PayPal.
