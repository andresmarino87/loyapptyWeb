Cookies

As is standard practice on many websites, the Site and Application use "cookies" to help us study traffic patterns on our Site and Application and understand the effectiveness of our marketing communications. A cookie is a small string of text that a website can send to your browser. We utilize two types of cookies as part of the Service. First, we utilize persistent cookies to save your Loyappty Account information for future logins to the Application, Site and Service and to integrate your usage of the Application, Site and Service with the appropriate SNS. Second, we utilize session ID cookies to enable certain features of the Application, Site and Service, to better understand how you interact with the Application, Site and Service and to monitor aggregate usage by Users and web traffic routing on the Application, Site and Service. Cookies can reveal, for instance, which pages on the Site and Application are the most popular, how much time visitors are spending there, and how they are interacting with the content on these pages. You have the option to disable cookies by changing the options in your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to view or use all portions of the Site and Application due to limited functionality.

Web Beacons

We may also use "web beacons," "pixel tags," "clear GIFs" or similar means (individually or collectively "Web Beacons") in our Application and on the Site. A Web Beacon is an electronic image, often a single pixel, embedded on web pages. Web Beacons are ordinarily not visible to users. Web Beacons allow us to count the number of users who have visited certain pages of the Application or Site, to deliver services and to generate statistics about how our Application and Site are used.
