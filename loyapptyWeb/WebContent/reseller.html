Loyappty Reseller Program

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Loyappty reseller! Here you will find the information you need to evaluate whether the program is a good fit for your plans.

The Opportunity

Businesses of all sizes are looking to incorporate mobile into their marketing initiatives.  They want to take advantage of mobile media to better interact with customers and prospects.
When thinking about mobile, businesses need to create persona, timely and engaging campaigns that help build the consumer relationship and grow customer loyalty.
Businesses build customer loyalty via Loyappty by:

  •  Staying in touch with current customers
  •  Sending coupons, offers, reminders, updates, and much more!
  •  Attracting new customers with timely and compelling offers

As of February 2012, nearly half (or about 49.7 percent) of U.S. mobile subscribers now own a smartphone, according to a new Nielsen report.

Program Details

As a Loyappty Reseller, you get:

  • Sales tools, training and support
  • Marketing programs, collateral, and tools
  • Reseller logo branding
  • A percentage of all revenues derived from customers you refer.
  • For more details, refer to the Loyappty Reseller Program Guide.

What is required of you?
As a representative of Loyappty and the Ogangi brand, Ogangi expects you to act with professionalism and integrity in your customer relationships. Ogangi expects you to:

  • Find and sell to businesses - the more businesses you reach, the more money you make.
  • Help register and get businesses started - this involves creating the businesses account, performing basic training, and assisting in the design and setup of the first loyappties.
  • Take any other rollout steps necessary to satisfactorily help businesses start loyappting.
  • For more details, refer to the Loyappty Reseller Program Guide.

Who is typically a good fit for this program?

Beyond the above requirements, experience shows that the most successful resellers fit the following profile:

  • Entrepreneurial spirit, flexibility and self-motivation with results driven attitude
  • Adopted and being a strong advocate of mobile media. Experience with smartphones and all forms of mobile media such as messaging, mobile Internet (browsing), and application downloads.
  • A work style that emphasizes a disciplined and organized approach to reaching out to several prospects at the same time, prioritizing opportunity efforts, and driving execution.
  • Understanding of business operational costs and impact of growing customer loyalty.

Businesses are looking to you for a pragmatic combination of mobile technology and marketing guidance. Loyappty recognizes and appreciates this trusted relationship, and would like to bring value to both you and your customers.
